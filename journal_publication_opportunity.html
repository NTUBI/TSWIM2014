JOURNAL PUBLICATION OPPORTUNITY

Authors of selected workshop papers will be invited to submit complete versions of their research for review and possible publication in a list of journals: ACM Transaction on Management Information Systems (http://tmis.acm.org/), Pacific Asia Journal of the Association for Information Systems (http://aisel.aisnet.org/pajais/), and Journal of Electronic Commerce Research (http://www.csulb.edu/journals/jecr/) (SSCI).
