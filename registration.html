Registration Requirement
In order for your paper to appear in the conference program and proceedings, at least one author for each paper must register for TSWIM 2014.

Registration commencing date is June 5, 2014. Please click the following link to register: http://goo.gl/UmKPjA. If you have any question regarding the online registration system or encounter any difficulty when using the system, please contact the conference registrar at 2014tswim@gmail.com

Registration Payment Information
Please click the following link to pay: http://140.117.13.70/pay/pay.asp. We offer 4 payment methods, including convenience store payment, ATM transfer, Bank of Taiwan Payment, and Credit Card. The payment deadline is June 25 but convenience store payment is June 20.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Can I register and pay onsite (at the conference)? A: Yes. The conference only accepts cash for onsite registrations.
However, we strongly encourage all participants to register and pay in advance. Early registration fees are offered at discounted rates.

Q: What is the last day for conference registration via the website? A: June 25, 2014. But convenience store payment is June 20, 2014.

Q: I have special dietary needs. How can I request special meals? A: We are happy to honor your special dietary needs. Please indicate your request on your registration form prior to the conference. All special dietary requests must be made prior to the conference itself; requests made on site may not be honored.

Q: How do I make a change to my registration? A: To amend your registration, please send an email to 2014tswim@gmail.com, indicating the changes to be made or call +886-7-525-2000 ext 4509 for assistance.
