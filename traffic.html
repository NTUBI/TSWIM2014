TRANSPORTATION 中文English

NSYSU locates at the scenic Sizihwan of Kaohsiung and is close to Kaohsiung MRT (KRTC) Sizihwan Station.

From Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)
1. Take the shuttle bus operated by UBUS to Taiwan High Speed Rail Taoyuan Station (NT$30).
2. Take THSR from Taoyuan Station to Zuoying Station (NT$1,455).
3. Take Kaohsiung MRT from Zuoying Station (R16) to Sizihwan Station (O1) (NT$35). (About 25mins)

From Kaohsiung International Airport (KHH)
Take Kaohsiung MRT from Kaohsiung International Airport Station (R4) to Sizihwan Station(O1) (NT$40). (About 30mins)

From THSR Zuoying Station
Take Kaohsiung MRT from Zuoying Station (R16) to Sizihwan Station (O1) (NT$35). (About 25mins)

From Taiwan Railway Kaohsiung Station
Take Kaohsiung MRT from Kaohsiung Main Station (R11) to Sizihwan Station (O1) (NT$20). (About 15mins)

By Car
Freeway No.1 → Jhong Jheng (Chung Cheng) Interchange →Jhong Jheng (Chung Cheng) Road → Connect to Da Gong Rd. → Left turn on Ci Sian (Chi Hsien) 3rd Rd. → Right turn on Ling Hai 1st Rd. → Connect to Lian Hai Rd. → National Sun Yat-sen University Campus.

◎Parking Area
By the Hsitzewan Beach, there is guest Parking. The fee is NT60 per day.
