HOTEL ACCOMMODATION

The following hotels (nearby National Sun Yat-sen University) are pleased to offer special rates for the participants of TSWIM 2014. Please note that only a limited number of rooms with special rates are available. Hence, please make your reservations earlier. To book rooms, please download the reservation form of your selected hotel and FAX or EMAIL your filled-in reservation form to the hotel DIRECTLY. Please remember to indicate that you are TSWIM 2014 participants.
